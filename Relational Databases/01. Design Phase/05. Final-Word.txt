Ignoring One to Many and Many to Many relationships will cause critical
problems, however, ignoring One to One and Extended One to One relationships
does not, it is optional to create One to One and Extended One to One
relationships.

Whenever you found a need for One to One or Extended One to One relationship
, you can choose as follows:
  - Break up the table and apply the relationship.
  - Leave the table as it is, it is not mandatory.
  - Use views to achieve the same result as applying the relationship.
